TEAM AQUA

Team Aqua is a group of businessmen who believe strongly in the use of Pokemon for the betterment of humans. For the past twenty years we have been building up from scratch a large stock of Pokemon from all around the world. Using teams of expert catchers and informants working together these pokemon are of the highest quality. Besides using these pokemon in the industrial scene Team Aqua has set up a market for the selling of pokemon. We are moving the world into the future, where the lives of humans are easy and carefree. For the good of all we work and despite protest from some fronts on our goals our work continues unabated.
